Admissions

We welcome students to join Veena Public School for the academic year 2024-25. Below are the details for the admission process:

  • Eligibility: Students aged 3 and above.
  • Process: Fill out the admission form available at the school office.
  • Documents Required: Birth certificate, Aadhar card, and previous school records (if applicable).
  • Important Dates: Admissions open from 1st November 2023 to 31st December 2023.
